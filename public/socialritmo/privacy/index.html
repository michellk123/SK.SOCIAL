1. Who we are

SocialRitmo is a tool by S.K Social for social media managers to plan and organize content for the client accounts they manage. This policy explains what data we handle, why, and how you stay in control of it.

2. What data we collect

Account data (managers): your name, email address, and optional profile photo and company name, used to create and operate your account.

Client records (created by managers): the brand name, optional email, optional logo, and Instagram handle you enter for each client you manage. This is information you already hold about your own clients.

Content you create: scheduled posts, captions, tasks, notes, and media (images/video) you upload to plan content. Uploaded media is stored with our media provider (Cloudinary) solely to display it back to you in the app.

Instagram data (only if you connect an account — see Section 3).

We do not sell personal data, and we do not use your data for advertising.

3. Instagram connection (Instagram API with Instagram Login)

Connecting a client’s Instagram account is optional. You connect it only when you want that client’s recent posts to appear in your planning grid. If you never connect an Instagram account, none of this section applies to you.

What happens when you connect:

  • You are sent to Instagram’s own login screen. We never see or receive the Instagram username or password — authentication happens entirely on Instagram.
  • Instagram returns an access token scoped to that one account, with the instagram_business_basic permission (read-only access to basic profile info and recent media). We request no publishing, messaging, or insights permissions.

What we store, and where:

  • The access token, encrypted at rest (authenticated symmetric encryption with a key held in a secrets vault). The token is held server-side only — it is never sent to or stored on your phone, and it is never exposed through our app.
  • The connected account’s Instagram user ID and the connection/expiry timestamps, used to keep the connection working and to refresh the token before it expires.

What we do with it:

  • We use the token only to read that account’s recent media (thumbnail image, link to the post, and post date) and show those thumbnails in your planning grid.
  • We fetch this media live when you open the client’s content screen. We do not store copies of the client’s Instagram posts; we display them from Instagram’s own URLs.

What we never do:

  • We never post, comment, message, like, or change anything on the connected account.
  • We never share the token or the account’s data with any third party.
  • We never use the data for advertising or profiling.

4. How long we keep Instagram data

  • The access token is retained until you disconnect the account in SocialRitmo, or until the connection lapses (Instagram access tokens expire after about 60 days; we attempt an automatic refresh, but if an account stays disconnected/inactive past expiry the token becomes invalid and is cleared).
  • When you disconnect, or when a user removes SocialRitmo from their Instagram settings, we delete the stored token and Instagram identifiers for that connection (see Section 6).

5. How we protect your data

  • Instagram access tokens are encrypted at rest and are accessible only to our secured server functions — never to the mobile app or to other users.
  • Access to each connection is scoped to the manager who created it; one manager cannot read another manager’s tokens or connected-account data.
  • We keep an internal audit record of connect / disconnect / refresh / deletion events for security and support. These records never contain access tokens.

6. Your controls and data deletion

You can remove a connected Instagram account at any time, in two ways:

  1. In SocialRitmo: open the client’s content screen and tap Disconnect. This immediately deletes the stored token and Instagram identifiers for that connection.
  2. In Instagram: go to your Instagram settings → Apps and Websites → remove SocialRitmo. Instagram notifies us, and we automatically delete the stored token and Instagram identifiers for the affected account.

To request deletion of your SocialRitmo account and all associated data, contact saraik.social@gmail.com. See our Data Deletion page.

7. Third-party processors

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and secured server functions (data hosting).
  • Cloudinary — storage/delivery of media you upload for content planning.
  • Instagram / Meta — only when you connect an Instagram account, as described in Section 3.

These providers process data on our behalf to operate the service. We do not authorize them to use your data for their own purposes.

8. Children

SocialRitmo is a professional tool not intended for anyone under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
